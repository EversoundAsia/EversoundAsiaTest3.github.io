About Us

Eversound Asia was established in 2015 in collaboration with John Adorney, an outstanding Los Angeles-based composer. After hearing John's heartfelt and evocative music, we were inspired to expand the influence of this music, and that of other artists with similar talents, to the Asian market

Company's Vision

Producing and marketing of quality music that would resonate with listeners globally. We hope to bridge the music culture of the East with the West to initiate a new era of music compilations.

Values and Propositions

One of Eversound Asia's key focus to work with artistes and corporate on very personal basis to create music that fully reflect their branding.
